Meet Our Team

"At STUDIO65, our dedicated team is led by two passionate individuals, Christopher Nolan and Ruther Ford. Christopher brings an artistic flair and a keen eye for detail to each photography project, while Ruther's technical expertise ensures that every shot is perfectly composed. Together, they form a dynamic duo committed to capturing the essence of your special moments. With their creative vision and unwavering dedication, Christopher and Ruther make your dreams come to life through the lens."

Hello, I'm Christopher Nolan, and I'm honored to share my journey as a wedding photographer with you. My love for photography ignited at a young age and grew into a lifelong passion. Over the years, I've had the privilege of documenting countless love stories, each one uniquely beautiful. With a background in visual storytelling and an unwavering commitment to capturing authentic moments, I strive to create timeless /images that speak to the heart. My career has been shaped by a dedication to exceeding my clients' expectations, turning their cherished memories into art. As a storyteller, I believe that every wedding is a tale waiting to be told through the lens, and I take great pride in preserving those moments that make your day unforgettable. My style is a blend of artistry, emotion, and a meticulous attention to detail, resulting in photographs that you'll treasure for generations to come. I look forward to the opportunity to work with you and capture the beauty and love that define your special day."
